Wei Zhao

Wei Zhao is a first-year PhD student at Technische Universität Darmstadt, Germany, working in AIPHES led by Iryna Gurevych. He received master degree in Computer Science at University of Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2018, worked with Min Yang and Yu Qiao. He was a visiting student at Nanyang Technological University in 2018, Singapore. He studied undergraduate in Linguistics at Fudan University and worked as NLP and IR researcher at Tencent, China. His research interests include Applied Machine Learning, NLP and Computational Linguistics. Please contact me if you are interested (andyweizhao1@gmail.com).
