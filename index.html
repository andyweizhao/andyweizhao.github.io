Wei Zhao

Wei Zhao will be a PhD student in TU Darmstadt starting from September, 2018. Prior to that, he was a researcher in Tencent, working on Multimodal Learning and NLP related tasks. He received master degree from University of Chinese Academy of Sciences, Shenzhen, advised by Prof. Min Yang and Prof. Yu Qiao. He received his undergraduate degree in Linguistics from Fudan University. His research interests include (but not limited) Natural Language Processing(e.g. text classification, dialog generation), Multimodal Learning (e.g., image captioning, video summarization) and Recommdation System. Let me know if interested (andyweizhao1@gmail.com)
